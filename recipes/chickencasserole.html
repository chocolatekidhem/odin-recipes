Chicken Casserole

This chicken casserole recipe belongs to P and has been passed down to Hannah through oral tradition. She can only hope to preserve its taste through this website.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 cream of chicken can
  • 1 sour cream 16oz
  • 1 stick butter
  • Ritz crackers
    •

    Steps

    1. Cut breasts and wash
    2. Boil chicken
    3. Cut chicken up
    4. Combine chicken, sour cream, and cream of chicken with some pepper and a little salt
    5. Add a little broth if you want
    6. Pour mixture into casserole dish
    7. Crumble crackers on top
    8. Melt butter and pour on top
    9. 300 degrees for..?
